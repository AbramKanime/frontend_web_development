Hi there! My name is Abraham, and welcome to my learning journal.

After months of learning of couple of programming languages on my own ranging from C, Python, and Javascript, I made a decision to focus and learn front-end web development because I realized that I had more passion for the creativity that comes with it. I then signed up to become a Scrimba student. This is going to cover my journey in this field of web development, and hopefully my story motivates someone out there.

Before venturing into the world of Web development, I have a background and career in Finance/Accounting and Data analytics. While in these fields, I was and still very good in what I did, but I did not feel a sense of fulfilment. I have always admired and in awe of the possiblities and creations in the world of tech. It always felt out of reach to me; until I changed my mindset. I have always known my capabilities, and that I am capable of so much more. So, after six years in the world of finance/accounting, I decided to venture into this world of tech. I can attest that this is one of the best decisions I have taken. It is never too late for a change and to pursue your dreams.

I love the fact that in this world of tech, everything is possible. It is a world of possibilities. The power to create is in your hands. I am here to make a mark
